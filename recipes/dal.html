Dal

Description

Dal is a dish made from dried lentils, peas, or beans, or the lentils themselves. It's a staple food in South Asia and is a rich source of protein and fiber.

What are the health benefits of dal?

  1. Dal is rich in protein, fiber, magnesium, calcium, B vitamins, and folate.
  2. The fiber in dal helps regulate blood sugar.
  3. Moong dal is easy to digest and can help with weight loss.
